Common Questions

You might find the answer to your question in our FAQ section

How do I create a skill listing?

After creating an account, go to your dashboard and click "Offer a Skill". Fill out the form with details about your skill and what you'd like in return.

Is there a rating system?

Yes, after each swap, both users can rate their experience. This helps build trust within the community.

Can I swap skills remotely?

Absolutely! Many skills can be exchanged virtually. Make sure to specify this in your listing.
