How SkillSwap Works

Learn how to start swapping skills with others in your community

1

Create Your Profile

Sign up and create a profile listing the skills you can offer and what you're looking for in return. Be specific about your expertise and what you hope to gain from the SkillSwap community.

2

Browse or Post Skills

Explore available skills in your area or post your own skill offering. You can search by category, location, or specific keywords to find exactly what you're looking for.

3

Connect with Other Users

Once you find a skill you need, message the user to discuss the details of the swap. Arrange a time, location, and duration that works for both parties.

4

Complete Your Skill Swap

Meet up (in person or virtually) and exchange your skills. Remember to be respectful of each other's time and expertise.

5

Rate Your Experience

After completing a swap, rate your experience and provide feedback. This helps build trust within the community and ensures quality exchanges for everyone.
